About Us

Document your classic car history

Our platform lets you register your vehicles and all the information about them. The owner of the car can register evidence and authorize other service providers and certifiers to do that as well. Authorization access can also be given to other users, such as potential buyers. Proper documentation of a classic vehicle can significantly increase its monetary value. Our work and its underlying technologies have gathered interest from several classic car restoration industry players, classic car owners, other workshops, and certification entities

Blockchain-based Decentralized Platform

We faciliate the adequate recording about classic car information, restoration procedures applied, and all all associated documentation by ensuring this information is immutable and trustworthy.

Moreover, this sytem provides data sharing and facilitates collaboration between interested entities.

We follow the FIVA Charter of Turin guidelines for the restoration procedures. All users, workshops and other entities using our platform MUST follow and be aware of these guidelines.

How ClassicsChain works?

As the name suggests, ClassicsChain is developed using the captivating blockchain technology and established on the principles of decentralized web platforms. We use Hyperledger Fabric as our permissioned blockchain platform, along with IPFS(InterPlanetary File System) to provide a decentralized media storage.
